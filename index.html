GOOD TO KNOW

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between Trophy and Non-Trophy slots?

Trophy Slot: Play on your own account. Trophies and save-file progress stay on your account.

Non-Trophy Slot: Play on our account. Trophies and save-file progress stay on our account. Occasional logouts may happen when account passwords expire. Please reach out through Messenger so we can provide the updated password.

What does “Not Available” mean?

The slot is currently rented. When provided, the date beside the status is the expected day it becomes available.

What is “Awaiting Deactivation”?

The rental has ended, but the current renter still needs to complete the account deactivation process before the slot can be released.

Why are some games marked “PS5 Only”?

Those titles do not have a PS4 version in the JotunDGPH catalog and will be hidden when the PS4 filter is selected.

What is 7 Days Initial Rent?

This applies to newly released games. The first rental must be under the Premium Plan for 7 days. After the initial rental, you may extend based on our regular rental rates.
